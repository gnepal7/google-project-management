# Introduction: Defining project goals, scope, and success criteria

# Determining project goals and deliverables
  + Project goal
    - Desired outcome of a project
  + Project deliverables
    - The products or services that are created for the customer client, or project sponsor.
    - What gets produced or presented ant the end of a task, event, or process
    - Help quantify and realize the impact of the project.

# How to set SMART goals
  SMART = Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, Time-bound
  + Specific
    - What do i want to accomplish?
    - Why is this a goal?
    - Who is involved
    - Where should be the goal be delivered?
    - To what degree?
  + Measurable
    - How much
    - How many 
    - How will I do it
    => Metrics 
    => Benchmarks :- points of reference
  + Attainable/Managable
    - Can it reasonably be reached?
    - How can it be accomplished?
  + Relevant
    - Does the goal make sense?
    - Is the goal worthwhile?
    - Is it the right time?
  + Time-bound
    - Deadline

# Introduction to OKRs
  + OKRs = Objectives + key results
  - OKRs combine a goal and metric to determine a measurable ouotcome.
  + Objective
    - Defines what needs to be achieved
    - Describes a desired outcome
  + Key results
    - The measurable outcomes that define when the objective has been met
  + Objective
    - Similar to goals
    - Describe a desired outcome
  + OKR level
    - Company level
      > 
    - Project level
      > Project-level OKRs need to align with and support both company and department-level OKRs.
