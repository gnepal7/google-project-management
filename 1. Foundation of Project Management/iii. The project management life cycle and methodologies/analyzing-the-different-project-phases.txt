# Phases in action: Initiating and planning
  + Initiate the project
    - Define project goal
    - Determine resources, people, software, vender, ...
    - Get project approval
  + Make a plan
    - Create a budget
    - Set the schedule
    - Extablish your team
    - Determine roles and responsibilities
    - Planning for risk in change
    - Establisg communication

# Phases in action: Executing and closing
  + Execute and complete
    - Manage the progress
    - Communication 
    - Make adjustments
  + Close the project
    - Ensure all tasks have been completed
    - Confirm acceptance of the project outcome
    - Reflects on lesson learned
    - Communicate results with stakeholders
    - Celebrete the completing project
    - Formally move to next one
