# Key project manager roles and responsibilities
  + Project management responsibilities
    - Planning and organizing
      . Make use of productivity tools
    - Managing tasks
    - Budgeting and controlling costs and other factors
      . Monitor and manage the budget 
      . Tracck issues and risks
      . Manage quality
      . Remove unforseen barriers

# Responsibilities that utilize interpersonal skills
  + Interpersonal skills which project manager should have
    1. Teaching and mentoring
    2. Building relationships
    3. Controlling change
    4. Communicating
    5. Empowering the team

# A project manager’s role within a team
  - A Project manager is not ofter the direct manager of the 
    people working on a project team
  - Hold team members accountable for their assigned tasks
  - Ensure that issues and risks are tracked and visible, and 
    establish escalation paths
  - Understand and help teammates to adopt the right workflows
    and projece management styles
  - Collaborate with other team as per the required for completing 
    the project efficiently and on time.

# Working with cross-functional teams
  + Managing cross-functional teams effectively requires tha project 
    manager to:
    . Clarify goals
    . Get team members with the right skills
    . Measure progress
    . Recognize efforts
