# Introduction: Organizational structure and culture

# Overview of Classic and Matrix structures 
  + Classic Structure
    - Functional or top-down structure
    - CEO has direct authority over several department managers
    - CEO, and .... responsible to upper lavel staff
  + Matrix Structure
    > Adjacent department
    > Direct higher-ups and stakeholders from other departments or programs
    > Similar to cross-functional

# A project manager's role within different organizational structures 

# How organizational structure impacts project management
  - Authority has to do with your ability to make decisions for the project that impact the organization.
  - In matrix structure, can have more manager. Cross-team.
  
# The role of a Project Management Office 
  + PMO = Project Management Office
  + What are the functions of a PMO?
    - Strategic planning and governance
    - Best practices
    - Common project culture
    - Resource management
    - Creation of project documentation, archives, and tools
